## 2. Clinical Decision Support Disclaimer (Medical Safe Harbor)

AI Healthcare System (including its ML classifiers, telemetry alarms, and AI wellness advisory modules) is a **Clinical Decision Support (CDS) tool**, NOT a substitute for professional medical judgment, diagnosis, or treatment. 

*   **Clinician Oversight**: Licensed medical professionals must review, verify, and assume sole responsibility for all diagnostic predictions, care plans, and advice generated by the software.
*   **Emergency Situations**: The software must not be used for primary diagnostic triage in life-threatening or emergency situations.
*   **Patient Disclaimer**: Automated patient-facing wellness plans include a mandatory footer: 
    > *Medical Disclaimer: This advisory is for informational purposes only. Consult a qualified clinician for diagnoses or emergencies.*

## 3. Limitation of Liability Clause

To protect against legal recourse if the software encounters bugs, connection failures, or database locking:

*   **Liability Cap**: The maximum aggregate liability of the developers/authors for any direct damages arising under the purchase of a perpetual license key or developer component shall be strictly capped at the **total amount actually paid** by the customer for that license or component.
*   **Indirect Damages**: Under no circumstances shall the authors be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, or consequential damages (including, but not limited to, loss of data, loss of business, or clinical downtime).
*   **Third-Party Telephony/API**: We assume zero liability for any failures or delays in third-party integrations (such as Twilio/VoIP telephony for vital alarm calls, local network setups, or local WebLLM browser caching).

## 4. EULA Consent Requirement

*   **Mandatory Acceptance**: All users must accept the End User License Agreement (EULA) before accessing clinical features. This acceptance is recorded with version tracking, timestamp, IP address, and user agent for audit compliance.
*   **Version Tracking**: When the EULA is updated, users must re-accept the current version before continued access to clinical endpoints.
*   **HTTP 451**: Unauthenticated access to clinical features returns HTTP 451 (Unavailable For Legal Reasons) until consent is recorded.

---

## 5. Data Encryption Obligations

*   **Encryption at Rest**: Protected Health Information (PHI) fields support Fernet symmetric encryption (AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256) via the `PHI_ENCRYPTION_KEY` environment variable.
*   **Encryption in Transit**: All production deployments must use TLS. HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) headers are enforced with `max-age=31536000`.
*   **Key Management**: Encryption keys must be rotated per organizational security policy. The system supports graceful key rotation with backward-compatible decryption.

---

## 6. Breach Notification Policy

In compliance with HIPAA §164.408 and GDPR Article 33:

*   **72-Hour Notification**: Upon discovering a breach involving Protected Health Information, the breach notification framework initiates a 72-hour countdown for regulatory notification.
*   **Incident Tracking**: All breach incidents are assigned unique IDs, severity classifications, and tracked through reporting → investigation → containment → notification → resolution stages.
*   **Audit Trail**: All breach reports, status updates, and containment actions are logged to the security audit trail.

AI Healthcare System implements technical safeguards aligned with the following regulatory frameworks:

| Framework | Jurisdiction | Key Requirements |

The HIPAA compliance self-check endpoint (`GET /v1/admin/compliance/hipaa`) provides a programmatic assessment of technical safeguard readiness.

---

## 8. AI Output Safety Limitations

*   **Confidence Thresholds**: AI predictions below the minimum confidence threshold (configurable, default 15%) are flagged with low-confidence warnings and require mandatory clinician verification.
*   **High-Risk Escalation**: Predictions classified as "high" or "critical" risk with moderate confidence trigger elevated caution warnings.
*   **Prompt Injection Protection**: All user inputs are screened for prompt injection attempts. Detected injections are blocked before reaching AI models.
*   **PII Redaction**: Output guardrails automatically redact 8 categories of personally identifiable information (email, SSN, Aadhaar, phone, credit card, passport, MRN, IP address) from AI-generated responses.
